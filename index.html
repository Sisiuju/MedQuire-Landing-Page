App Features

Everything you need for peace of mind

Designed to be simple, medically accurate, and completely focused on patient understanding.

Drug Search with Autocomplete

Type the first few letters of any prescription or over-the-counter medicine to find its details instantly.

ELI12 Simplification Mode

Confused by complex medical terms? Tap the ELI12 toggle to translate labels into friendly child-like analogies.

Personal Medicine Cabinet

Save active prescriptions to your device for quick access, refill deadlines, and localized warnings.

Drug Interaction Checker

Instantly highlights safety red flags and dangerous combination alerts before taking multiple medications.

Exportable Summary Card

Generate a neat, shareable summary card of your medications to show doctors or print for family members.

Target Audience

Built for everyone who takes medicine

Health literacy shouldn't require a medical degree. MedQuire helps make health safe and accessible for all.

👴

Prescription Managers

Adults managing multiple daily medications for chronic conditions like high blood pressure, cholesterol, or diabetes who want clarity on safety guidelines.

👩‍👧‍👦

Caregivers

People managing care details for elderly parents or children who need a quick reference to check drug mixtures, dosages, and side effects.

🗣️

Non-Native Speakers

Individuals who speak English as a second language and struggle with advanced clinical terminology in medical pamphlets.

📘

Low-Literacy Users

Patients who find typical medical jargon confusing and want straightforward, digestible guidance and answers.
